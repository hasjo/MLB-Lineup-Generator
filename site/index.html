The Scorecard Report Site

A Spot For Information

Hey folks! This is a site where I'm hosting the automatically generated starting lineup reports that I've been using for my scorecard keeping while watching Baseball games. I've got this site organized into two sections, pages and receipts. This is because I use a receipt printer to print out my lineups for ease of copying to my card.

I realize that this isn't something that everyone has access to, so I generate printer-ready pages in the pages section. If you also are silly and have a receipt printer on hand, the receipts section has generated pdfs for 80mm receipts.
